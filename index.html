Delete your Wanderly.Ai account

You can permanently delete your account from within the app or by contacting us.

In‑app steps (recommended):

  • Open the app ➝ Menu ➝ Delete Account.
  • Confirm the request in the dialog. You may be asked to re‑authenticate.
  • We will process the deletion promptly; see timelines below.

Email request (alternative):

  • Email wanderly.ai.app@gmail.com from your registered email.
  • Subject: “Delete my Wanderly.ai account”.
  • Include: your full name, registered email, and (if available) your user ID (UID).

What will be deleted

  • Your authentication account (Firebase Auth).
  • Your profile document in our database (e.g., name, email, flags).
  • Questionnaire/preferences you provided.
  • In‑app generated content (e.g., saved itineraries or similar profile‑tied data).
  • Corresponding backend profile records.

What may be retained temporarily

  • Security, fraud‑prevention, and operational logs for up to 90 days.
  • Data retained to meet legal or compliance obligations, where applicable.
  • Aggregated/anonymized analytics that cannot identify you.

Request deletion of specific data (without deleting your account)

If you prefer to keep your account but remove certain data (for example, questionnaire responses or saved items), email wanderly.ai.app@gmail.com with details of the data to remove. We’ll confirm once completed.

Verification & timelines

  • We may request identity verification to protect your account.
  • We aim to complete requests within 7–30 days. You’ll receive an email confirmation.

Questions

Contact wanderly.ai.app@gmail.com. Please also review our Privacy Policy for more details on data practices.
